Culture and Architecture

Polish Legacy in Georgian Architecture

Tbilisi, the vibrant capital of Georgia, hides a fascinating legacy of Polish architecture waiting to be discovered. From elegant residential buildings to historic public structures, Polish architects left a lasting mark on the city in the 19th and early 20th centuries. Walking through these streets, visitors can admire stories behind the architects and their creations. Explore Tbilisi through the eyes of Polish heritage and experience a unique cultural journey.

Hike and Nature

Tobavarchkhili Lakes

Tobavarchkhili Lakes, nestled in the lush mountains of Georgia, offer a serene escape for nature lovers and adventurers alike. Surrounded by dense forests and dramatic cliffs, these pristine waters reflect the sky and create breathtaking landscapes. Visitors can hike along scenic trails, enjoy picnics by the shore, or simply relax in the tranquility of the area. Discover Georgia's hidden gem and experience a peaceful retreat immersed in unspoiled natural beauty.
